Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our platform and services

How quickly can I find a donor in an emergency?
Our AI matching system typically connects recipients with compatible donors in under 15 minutes for critical emergencies. The average response time for urgent cases is 45 minutes.
What safety measures are in place for donors and recipients?
All donors undergo comprehensive health screening. Our platform verifies donor identities and medical histories. All connections are made through certified medical facilities to ensure safety.
How does the AI matching algorithm work?
Our algorithm analyzes blood type compatibility, location proximity, donor availability, donation history, and urgency level to find the optimal match in real-time.
Is there a cost to use BloodBank Connect?
Our platform is completely free for recipients seeking donors in emergency situations. Donors are never charged. We operate through partnerships with hospitals and health organizations.
