"I believe technology can save lives—especially when it’s built by those who understand the real challenges."
Hello! I’m Ankit Kumar, a passionate coder from Saran District (Chapra), Bihar. Living in a college hostel, I’ve seen first-hand how difficult it can be to arrange emergency blood donations—especially in rural and semi-urban areas where there’s no quick access to donors or a centralized system.

That’s why I created BloodBank Connect: a platform that connects patients and donors faster, smarter, and more reliably. My goal is to use technology to solve real problems that impact people around me.

This project is a showcase of my front-end skills—HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Supabase for authentication and real-time data. I’m also integrating AI (Gemini API) for intelligent blood group compatibility, making the platform not just functional, but life-saving.

I believe that even as students, we can make a real difference in society through code. I’m always learning, improving, and dreaming bigger. My hope is to expand BloodBank Connect into a tool that helps people across India in urgent medical need.
